    The first case of the COVID-19 pandemic in the Indian state of Karnataka was confirmed on 8 March 2020. Two days later, the state became the first in India to invoke the provisions of the Epidemic Diseases Act, 1897, which are set to last for a year, to curb the spread of the disease.[2] As of 30 May 2021, Karnataka have 2587827 confirmed cases and 28679 deaths.[3] with 2217117 recovered cases and 342010 active cases.
